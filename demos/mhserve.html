about us

Welcome to HANUVA MARINE SERVICES

Hanuva Marine Service Pvt Ltd incorporated in the south indian city, Chennai with experienced technical engineers. We are focused to strive for our customers success with dedicated service includes great technical skills, good workmanship. We are totally committed to deliver the quality products and services to our customers at competitive price. Our motto is to build very good business relationship with all our customers and grow-up together with upgraded technologies and quality of services.

We are specialized in repairing, servicing, trouble-shooting, retrofitting, Installation of new system, Calibration and annual service for Electrical, Pneumatic and Hydraulic control systems equipments Onboard ship. We also do planning, design, development, implementation and commissioning of various control systems as per customer requirement.
